Bark!

Bark! is our WAD2 team's pet project, a student-friendly discussion board.

It's quite simple, you can search the board if you have a question or sniff around by tags, and if you can't retrieve an answer then just BARK! You can also comment.

If you have any questions you can hound us on GitHub, or just make a post with your problem and we'll see it! Alternatively, if you dig it, please also let us know!
